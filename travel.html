The Travel page

I don't travel much unless it's to eat

This is a list of places I've travelled to and loved around Ann Arbor (I love food so they're all food places lol)




Ashley's on S State St


Best food to accompany your beer.


KouZina on N Main St


Best greek street food, but this is the only greek street food I've ever had so...


OBD's Mongolian Grill on Ford Rd, Canton


Best mongolian food ever, I'm hungry even thinking about it.


Möge Tee on Plymouth Rd


Best beverages, all creamy and sweet and actually nice tasting.


Shalimar on S Main St


Nice indian food but I can make better haha.
